Blood on the Clocktower Trouble Brewing

Blood on the Clocktower is a social deduction game published by the Pandemonium Institute. Players are assigned different character roles, each providing unique abilities, and must decipher who has what role.


Good players want to find and eliminate the Evil players, while the Evil players want to eliminate enough Good players to win.


There are various editions of the game which can be purchased and played physically or online.


Navigate to discover each role in the beginner-level “Trouble Brewing” edition!
